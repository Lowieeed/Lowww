01 — The Vision & Narrative

An Ancient Emperor.
A Mythic Event.

The brief demanded a skin launch trailer that matched the production quality of top-tier mobile MOBA marketing — without looking like anyone else's work.

The creative decision was immediate: frame this not as a game advertisement, but as a cinematic event. An ancient dragon emperor does not launch. He awakens.

Three acts across 15 seconds: Emergence → Power → Dominance. Every frame earns its place in the sequence. Nothing is decorative.

02 — Visual Development & Style Frames

The Color System

Every color in the palette carries a narrative function. Black is the void the emperor rises from. Gold is power restrained. Crimson is aggression and skill energy. Ember orange is the trail of destruction left behind.

The palette was locked before a single frame was produced. Discipline in the color system is what makes the final composite feel inevitable rather than assembled.
